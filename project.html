Doctor_Appointment_Booking

EasyBooking revolutionizes appointment scheduling, offering slots for audio/video calls over the next 7 days. Users effortlessly select their desired time and call type, receiving notifications via direct message or email. With reminder alerts, missed appointments become a thing of the past. Secure user authentication ensures privacy, while personalized profiles streamline communication. Seamlessly manage appointments across devices thanks to the app's responsive design. Users can easily join calls and integrate their schedules with their calendar apps, maximizing efficiency. EasyBooking prioritizes simplicity and convenience, making it the go-to choice for hassle-free booking of audio and video calls.
